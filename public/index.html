Our Mission

Sunshine Farm Philippines aims to give hope to persons with disabilities and it wants to prove the world that disabilities doesn't mean incapabilities. Its goal is to grow and sell flowers to hire PWDs and provide a sustainable income to the said community.

Sunflower farm in Quezon plants seeds of hope for PWDs

QUEZON, Philippines – More than just a business, a sunflower farm in Tiaong, Quezon empowers persons with disabilities (PWDs) by providing sustainable livelihood. “We need to change the mindset that persons with disabilities are limited and that they can’t do anything,” said Sunshine Farm owner Rhodora Palomar-Fresnedi. Farm manager Ben Villanueva, 44, who has polio, said his earnings from the Sunshine Farm helps him send his child to school. The farm also helps improve the lives of PWDs in Tiaong
